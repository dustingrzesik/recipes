Krishna Lunch Dressing

Easy to find ingredients:
2 ½ cups almonds
2 ½ cups water
2 ½ cups corn oil
2 teaspoons salt

Harder to find ingredients:
2 heaping teaspoons asafetida [a pungent spice used in Indian cooking] (it smells like rotten eggs and is key to the dressing.)
3 ½ teaspoons Bragg's (hippie food store for this liquid "amino acid")
2 cups nutritional yeast (available as yellow flakes in bulk from hippie food store)

Instructions:
Toss in a blender and blend till desired consistency
Add water to thin out if you have trouble pouring it
